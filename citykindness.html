City Kindness is a community focused partnership seeking to serve some of the most marginalised people in Berlin. This partnership is made possible with the generous support of volunteers from corporate and non-profit sectors working together to express kindness towards others.

City Kindness seeks to support underprivileged children, families in need (including single parents and families seeking refuge) as well as the elderly.

ABOUT



Futureminded Group is committed to finding and supporting positive solutions to the challenges Berlin faces now and in the future.


We believe that the future of Berlin can be shaped positively by the resourcefulness of the people that call Berlin their home.
